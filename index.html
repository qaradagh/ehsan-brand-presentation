Core Concept

Personal initials shaped by market logic.

The identity combines the initials E and F with a visual language inspired by price structure, vertical levels, and directional movement through liquidity zones.

E

Hidden Initial

The lower structure reveals the letter E as a geometric foundation, grounding the mark in Ehsan's personal identity.

F

Ascending Identity

The upper structure forms the letter F, rising above the diagonal axis as a symbol of clarity, direction, and execution.

LIT

Liquidity Movement

The diagonal cut is not a generic growth arrow. It represents price movement between meaningful liquidity areas.

Logo System

Built on contrast, rhythm, and precision.

The mark balances vertical stability with diagonal momentum. This gives the identity a controlled but dynamic personality suitable for analytical trading.

01 / Vertical Structure

Discipline and Market Levels

The vertical forms suggest levels, candles, and measured structure. They create a sense of control rather than randomness.

02 / Diagonal Motion

Price Path and Transition

The diagonal axis introduces momentum and separates the composition into two zones: foundation and activation.

03 / Visual Memory

A Discoverable Monogram

The EF combination is intentionally subtle, giving the logo a second layer that makes it more memorable during presentation.
